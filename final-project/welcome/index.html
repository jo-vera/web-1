What is the Egg Theory?

The Egg Theory, popularized by Andy Weir's short story "The Egg," proposes that:

  • We are all the same soul experiencing life in a linear fashion through every human being who has ever existed.
  • Upon your death, you are reborn into another life, your memories remain intact.
  • You are not just experiencing life, you are creating it. We are every single person who has ever lived and ever will live.
