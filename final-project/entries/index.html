The Originator

The date is July 6th, 2009 and I am named Andres. I am 27 years old and I will die soon. Exactly 3 years ago  I ran into a writer named Andy. I was on my way to work and had stopped by to grab a coffee first. Andy had mistaken my latte for his own, we laughed at the fact that our names were so similar, anyone could make that mistake. We said our goodbyes until we realized that we were walking in the same direction, we worked in the same building. Through many conversations we became friends and 2 years ago I felt the end was near. Over drinks at my house I unloaded the burden I carried so closely, I told Andy about the Egg. I told him about every past life I had lived or even remembered, I told him that I would soon be back in an unrecognizable state. I expected the worst reaction from him, I didn’t know whether I’d be believed, he sat there in silence until finally he just said, “wow.” Soon the world will know of the Egg.

My name is Eliza, I am 9 years old and the date is January 17th, 2022. I wanted to come back here quicker but my last life was cut short. I started to remember Andres around 5 months ago, I couldn’t figure out the login and I didn’t have a phone. I have an odd feeling in the pit of my stomach, usually even when I realize what I am, I remain hopeful for a future where I can experience a full life but this time it’s different. I felt it shortly before I died in 2011, something is off. I won’t die for many, many years again and I wonder if this feeling will ever go away.

My name is Orla and I am 17 years old, the year is 2069. I lived a longer life the last time. I woke up two days ago with a feeling as if I was fading. In 2042 Eliza had found more people who knew about the Egg, the Egg is getting weaker and more people are uniting and awakening. Are we finally becoming our one-self?

I am still Orla and it’s 2075, yesterday I met a young boy named Yuki. He stared at me for so long before speaking, he relayed a series of information he had learned that half of his hometown in Okinawa had awakened to the Egg, they found my webpage and have written their accounts.
