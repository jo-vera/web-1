Two Views

Author's View: How Graphic Design Is Evolving

Technology has drastically changed in graphic design. Graphic Design has moving it beyond just product packaging to online content creation for marketing and customer interaction. Companies are now using data and analytics to see what designs are most effective. This shift has led to a rise in content marketing and the importance of graphic design in digital strategies. The tools available have also evolved which allows designers to work remotely and companies to hire freelancers globally. This has opened up the field and made it more accessible for junior designers.

My Response

I appreciated the outlook of this article, this article was written in 2017 and much has changed since. I wonder if the author could have imagined how fast AI and other technologies evolved, did they think it would take much longer than 7 years? I do believe that graphic design still holds power in the marketing field, people seem to appreciate the art behind campaigns. There are many new paths for graphic designers and many people have found new paths to take.With the evolution of design tools, I wonder how much graphic design will change in another 7 years.
